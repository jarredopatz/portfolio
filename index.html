I’m Jarred Opatz, a seasoned editor and public relations professional with a passion for journalism.

About Me

I have 30 years of journalism experience in the newspaper industry, starting in sports and moving to news and editing. My first full-time job in the business was with the Red Wing Republican Eagle in Minnesota. I have worked at the Ashland Times-Gazette in Ohio since 1996 and both the T-G and The Daily Record in Wooster, Ohio since 2016. Throughout my 30-year career, I have developed strong writing, reporting and editing skills, particularly with feature writing. I also have won several awards for articles I have written.
